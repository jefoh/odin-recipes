Vegan Buffalo Mac 'N Cheese

Vegan mac n cheese with buffalo sauce, tempeh, and cashew "cheese"

This is a delicious recipe that is great for everyone. It's dank.

  • Cashews
  • Spices
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Garlic
  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Buffalo Tempeh
  1. Soak Cashews in water for at least 3 hours. Drian water and add to food processor.
  2. Add Spices, nutrional yeast, avacado to food processor and blend.
  3. Saute tempeh until browned, saute onions until translucent, add garlic and saute for a minute
  4. Boil water and cook macaroni. Drain.
  5. Combine sauce with tempeh, onion, garlic and sauce. Add buffalo sauce
