Garlic Bread

Who doesnt like garlic bread?

This vegan garlic bread is inspired by Guy Fieri

Ingredients

  • Vegan butter
  • 4 bulbs of garlic
  • Spices
  • Basil
  • Vegan mayo
  • French bread
  1. Cut of the top of the garlic bulb so the cloves are exposed. Season with salt, pepper and olive oil. Roast in oven for 50 minutes to an hour.
  2. Cut french bread in half lengthwise
  3. Mix together softened butter, mayo and spices in a bowl
  4. Once Garlic is roasted and soft, squeeze the cloves out of the bulbs in the same bowl as the butter mixture. Mix.
  5. Spread the mixture on both half bread slices. Garnish with basil.
  6. Put in broiler on low for a few minutes until bread edges are browned and crispy.
